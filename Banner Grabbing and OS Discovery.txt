Banner Grabbing

There are a few tools for banner grabbing

telnet and netcat

If version is not shown within nmap scan you can try to do a banner grap utilizing telnet and netcat
Another way to do this is try to log into the system, such as ssh
ex:
	nc 10.10.10.1 22 
	This will do a banner grap of port 22 on that IP address utilizing netcat

OS Fingerprinting

You can use NMAP

Another tool you can use is P0f
	./p0f -i eth0
